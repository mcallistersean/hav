## Accessiblity

The HAV attempts to make the contents of this website as widely and easily accessible as possible. We are also committed to the [general guidelines on accessibilty](https://www.univie.ac.at/en/about-us/accessibility/) as stipulated by the University of Vienna.

Should you have problems in accessing particular contents on our website, please contact us at [hav@univie.ac.at](mailto: hav@univie.ac.at).
